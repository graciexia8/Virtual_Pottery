Ji Zhou Ware

3000 - 2000 BC

Jizhou ware or Chi-chou ware (Chinese: 吉州窯) is Chinese pottery from Jiangxi province in southern China; the Jizhou kilns made a number of different types of wares over the five centuries of production. The best known wares are simple shapes in stoneware, with a strong emphasis on subtle effects in the dark glazes, comparable to Jian ware, but often combined with other decorative effects.
