Qing Bai Ware

3000 - 2000 BC

Qingbai ware (青白 qīngbái) is a type of Chinese porcelain produced under the Song Dynasty & Yuan dynasty. Qingbai ware is white with a blue-greenish tint. It was made mostly in Jiangxi province, and is arguably the first type of porcelain to be produced on a very large scale. However, it was used mostly used for burial wares and exports.
